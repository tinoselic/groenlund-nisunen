LA BIBI
Palma
2022

Falling Water, Vertical Stripes in White, Solar Incidences, Reflection, Pair, Led-Panels

A solo exhibition arranged by La Bibi Gallery in Palma de Mallorca in September 2022.

The gallery has its permanent showroom also in Palma, but wanted to do this exhibition in an alternative space in the very centre of the city. The opening took place during the professional days of the Nuit de l'Art event. In 20220, we participated in art fairs in London and Madrid with La Bibi Gallery, and are planning a solo exhibition, which was postponed from 2022 to 2023, due to the Covid-19 pandemic.
