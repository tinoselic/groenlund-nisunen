Revolution Per Minute
3rd Moscow Biennial of Contemporary Art, Red October, Moscow
2009

Carbon-fibre rods, electric motors with reduction gear, bearings, axles

Eight white rods revolved slowly just above the floor in a former factory hall. The 1 rpm rotation was caused by electronic rotary motors placed on the floor. The rods' direction of rotation varied.
