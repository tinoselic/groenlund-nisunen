Freq_out 6
Dreamlands Burn Nordic Art Show - 2006, Mucsarnok Kunsthalle, Budapest
2007

Curated by Edit Molnár and Livia Páldi

Freq_out is a sound installation comprised of 12 individual sound works each utilizing a specific frequency range, made on site, and amplified to act as a single, generative sound-space.

The artists participating in freq_out 4 are:

0-25 Hz - Jana Winderen 25-65 Hz - Maia Urstad 65-90 Hz - Brandon LaBelle 90-140 Hz - Tommi Grönlund/Petteri Nisunen 140-180 Hz - Finnbogi Pétursson 180-250 Hz - Franz Pomassl 250-350 Hz - B J Nilsen 350-500 Hz - Jacob Kirkegaard 500-1000 Hz - Mike Harding 1000-2000 Hz - Kent Tankred 2000-5000 Hz - J.G. Thirlwell 5000-11000 Hz - PerMagnus Lindborg
