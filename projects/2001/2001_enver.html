Enver – Wireless Composer Robot
Astra Biotech Laboratory, Södertälje
2001

Enver is making a new random composition of 400 steel bearings every day. Enver is operating tirelessly on a vertical steel wall with magnetic wheels, electronic motors, rechargable battery and electronic eye and an arm to grip.

He's working from 8 to 5 along with the other laboratory workers. From 8 to 12 he builds up a new composition and from 12 to 5 takes the balls back to a storage behind the wall. In night Enver sleeps and recharges himself.
