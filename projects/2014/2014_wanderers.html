Wanderers
Kastelli Community Centre, Oulu
2014

Mirrors

Kiertolaiset / Wanderers is a permanent installation at Kastelli School and Multipurpose Hall in Oulu, Finland. It consists of 365 pcs of Ø28cm mirrors hanging on the walls of the light opening structure above the lunch room of Kastelli school. On sunny days the mirrors cast slowly moving light spots on the floor and the walls depending on the inclinaton of the sun.
