Catch
Serlachius Museums, Mänttä
2023

Catch is a group exhibition curated by Laura Kuurne and Eeva Ilveskoski, currently on display in Serlachius Museum Gösta in Mänttä.

We made a new commissioned work Falling for the exhibition, and besides that also our work Wave of Matter, 2012, is included in the exhibition.




Falling


A site-specific installation

Stepper motors, controllers, steel rack, stainless steel wire, acrylic counter weights, steel pool, distilled water
